Synopsis: 
Cybersecurity Career Week will take place on October 16-21, 2023, and you’re invited to participate! Join this webinar to learn more about #CyberCareerWeek to promote the discovery of cybersecurity careers. We will share topics, example activities, and advice on what you can do to prepare for and participate in Cybersecurity Career Week 2023.
