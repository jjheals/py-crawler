Nuria MartinezTeam B Leader, NIST Laboratories ProgramsGrants Management DivisionNISTSynopsis:On March 25, 2024, NIST announced funding for up to fifteen Regional Alliances and Multistakeholder Partnerships to Stimulate (RAMPS) cybersecurity education and workforce development. The RAMPS programs will establish community-based partnerships and develop cybersecurity career pathways that address local workforce needs. This webinar will provide information regarding the funding opportunity, offer general guidance on preparing applications, and answer questions.Resources:2024 RAMPS Notice Of Funding Opportunity (NOFO) Announcement Grants.gov RAMPS NOFO Application InformationRAMPS ProgramsNISTIR 8287: A Roadmap for Successful Regional Alliances and Multistakeholder Partnerships to Build the Cybersecurity WorkforceFrequently Asked Questions
