FULL WORKSHOP DETAILSNIST will host a workshop on the development of a new block cipher mode of operation on June 20-21, 2024, at the National Cybersecurity Center of Excellence in Rockville, Maryland. Workshop Announcement/Call for Abstracts (PDF)Important Dates Workshop: June 20-21, 2024  Submission deadline: May 1, 2024  Notification date: May 17, 2024  Registration deadline: June 13, 2024 Updates and additional information will be posted to the ciphermodes-forum email and to the workshop website. Instructions for subscribing to the email forum can be found at https://csrc.nist.gov/Projects/block-cipher-techniques/email-list-ciphermodes-forum.Inquiries:  ciphermodes [at] nist.gov (subject: BCM%20Workshop%20Question)  (ciphermodes[at]nist[dot]gov)
